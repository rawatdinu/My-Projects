<td><br /> <strong>Members</strong><br /> About 80 % of the European paper sack manufacturers, situated in 25 different European countries, co-operate in Eurosac today. Eurosac also counts 22 sympathising members who are suppliers to the paper sack industry like paper, machine, ink or glue manufacturers.</td>

<p><strong>Aim</strong><br /> Set up in 1952, EUROSAC is an Association between the European manufacturers of multiwall paper sack manufacturers for the promotion and development of the paper sack industry in Europe.</p>
